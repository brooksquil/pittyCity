Meet the Parents

Paris - The Violet Mother

Paris is the most affectionate dog in existence! She will smother you in cuddles and kisses. She has been the most meticulous mother, always grooming and caring for her babies.

This beautiful girl is beyond spoiled! She loves to run, play, and swim with other dogs. She has a medium drive but is really food motivated. Loves to snuggle and lay on pillows like a human!

Paris is a reliable protector and loyal loving dog; she has made life much more enjoyable for me! I know her pups will do the same for others.

Her muscles shows in her neck and chest. Incessant lover. Loves people and small dogs. Trainable and intelligent.

FAQS

  • Muscle Bound: Her muscles shows in her neck and chest.
  • Incessant lover: Loves people and small dogs.
  • Trainable and intelligent.

Stats:

  • 7 purple ribbons in her pedigree
  • UKC papered
  • 2x shamrock daughter/ Lucky Luciano

Dex - The Piebald Merle Father

Dex is a dog with a lot of love to give. He came to this kennel at 5 weeks and has developed into a super star.

He loves to exercise, play tug of war, and hang out the window of the truck! Not only does his temperament match his quirky style, but his love for socializing with other dogs really defines his personality.

He loves to snuggle, greet EVERYONE he meets, and relax with a good bone after a long day!

FAQS

  • Muscle Bound
  • Very sociable and playful
  • Trainable and intelligent.

Stats:

  • ABKC Registered
